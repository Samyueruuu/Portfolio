About Me

Hi, I’m Samuel Rouell M. Mazon, a recent Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate from Marinduque State University, majoring in Software Development. I’m passionate about creating efficient, user-friendly solutions through web development, as well as solving real-world IT problems with both hardware and software troubleshooting.

I have hands-on experience as a Freelance Web Designer and Freelance Technician, where I designed responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and provided technical support for computer hardware, software, and network systems. I also completed my internship as an IT Support Intern at the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR), where I worked on hardware/software troubleshooting, system maintenance, database updates, and fiber wiring installation.

I enjoy tackling challenges that combine creativity with technical problem-solving. With skills in web development, UI/UX design, and IT support, I aim to grow further in the tech industry while contributing to innovative and impactful projects.

Outside of work, I’m continuously learning and experimenting with new tools and technologies to sharpen my skills as both a developer and IT professional.
